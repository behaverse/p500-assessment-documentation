XCIT_TH_03

02 with constant sequence ABA

Duration: 10 minutes

About

Participants are asked to hold their mouse pointer on a disk and upon seeing a target (in either of two predefined positions) move the disk to that position. The order of highlighted target positions is switched predictably with ABA pattern.

Notes

constant sequence ABA

Instructions

1

Catch the star with the disk.

2

4

5
Let's do a few examples!
