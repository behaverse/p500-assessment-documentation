Instructions

1

Catch the star with the disk but avoid the red circle.

2
You will see a dark circle at the bottom of the screen. Grab it by clicking on it and keeping the mouse button pressed without moving.
3
You will then see a star appear on one of the white circles shown on the screen. Sometimes, you will also see a red circle appear around the star.
4
Your job is to move your mouse and reach the star while keeping the mouse clicked, but only when there is no red circle around the star.
5
If a red circle appears around the star, stay away from it. Stop moving the mouse and release the mouse button.
6
Let's do a few examples!

Rules

Catch the star with the disk but avoid the red circle.
You will see a dark circle at the bottom of the screen. Grab it by clicking on it and keeping the mouse button pressed without moving. You will then see a star appear on one of the white circles shown on the screen. Sometimes, you will also see a red circle appear around the star. Your job is to move your mouse and reach the star while keeping the mouse clicked, but only when there is no red circle around the star. If a red circle appears around the star, stay away from it. Stop moving the mouse and release the mouse button. Don't wait for the red circle to appear! Try to hit the star as fast as you can and try stop your movement when the red circle appears. It's OK if you succeed only half of the time.
