XCIT_WO_03

Bivalent Shape Test

Duration: 15 minutes

About

Participants are asked to sort the central symbol based on its shape. Colors and symbols vary in congruent, incongruent, or neutral trials.

Adaptive — difficulty adjusts to the participant’s performance.

What it measures

  • accuracy (per congruent, incongruent, and neutral conditions)
  • response time (per congruent, incongruent, and neutral conditions)
