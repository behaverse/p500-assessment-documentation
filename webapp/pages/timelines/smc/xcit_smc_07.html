XCIT_SMC_07

mental rotation version: arrows + global panel rotation without transfo. helper

Duration: 11 minutes

About

Two sets of symbols are presented inside two boxes on the left and right side of the screen. Participants are tasked to report if the two sets are identical. Symbols that rotate or mirror are considered identical. Participants will be shown a helper that shows which item was rotated or mirrored.

What it measures

  • response time
  • accuracy

Notes

Mental rotation task without helper

Rules

Are the two images the same except for the rotation?
You will see two images on the screen that contain arrows. The image on the left is either exactly the same as the image on the right, once it is rotated appropriately, or there is exactly one arrow that is different between the two images. Your job is to determine if the two images are exactly the same or not, irrespective of the rotation. If the arrows are the same in both images, click on the button (=). If they are different, click on the button (≠). Respond as fast as you can without making too many errors. You must respond before the timer runs out.
