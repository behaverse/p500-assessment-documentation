Instructions

1

Track the moving diamonds.

2
You will see white dots on the screen. Some contain a diamond, and some are empty. You will briefly be shown where the diamonds are hidden before all dots start moving across the screen.
3
Your job is to keep track of where all the diamonds are.
4
After all the dots have stopped moving, you will be asked to select all the dots that contain a diamond by clicking on them.
5
Try to make as few errors as possible.
6
This test contains 25 trials.
7
You are now ready for the test. Press OK to start.
