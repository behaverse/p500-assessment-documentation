XCIT_UFOV_03

central + periphery - distractors

Duration: 8 minutes

About

A symbol appears on the central fixation point and another on one of the eight location around the fixation point along with distractors and participants are tasked to identify the symbol on the center and report where peripheral symbol appeared. There will be no peripheral distractor.

Notes

central + peripheral detection without distractors

Instructions

1

2

Identify the symbol and locate the star.

3
You will see a circle on the screen with a + in the center. After a brief time, a symbol will be shown and hidden in the center of the circle, and at the same time a star will be shown and hidden somewhere on that circle.
4
There are only two different symbols.
5
Your job is to say both which symbol was shown and where the star was.
6
After the symbol and the star disappear, click on the button that corresponds to the symbol you saw and the button that matches where you saw the star.
7
Keep your eyes focused on the screen center when there is a +.
8
Let's do a few examples!
