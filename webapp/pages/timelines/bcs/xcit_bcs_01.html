Instructions

1
This test has 3 parts and should take about 36 minutes to complete. Each part will be clearly indicated and there will be several breaks along the way. Press OK to start part 1/3.
2

Figure out the rules.

3
You will see a card and several buttons on the screen. Each time you click on a button, a new card will be shown.
4
Your job is to learn which button to click for each card.
5
You will be told after each response whether your choice was correct or not.
6
This test contains 64 trials.
7
You are now ready for the test. Press OK to start.
