XCIT_OOO_01

cambridge-like: staircase difficulty, as fast as you can

Duration: 8 minutes

About

Nine boxes are briefly shown in a 3x3 gird, and participants are tasked to identify one box that cannot be grouped with the other boxes based on its shape, color, and/or number of symbols.

Adaptive — difficulty adjusts to the participant’s performance.

Notes
